Group Estate Planning Workshops by Ross Estate Law

Our workshops make estate planning more accessible and affordable for communities across Nova Scotia. Designed for unions, workplaces, churches, and non-profits, these group sessions guide participants through a simple questionnaire to begin drafting their estate plans. After the workshop, Ross Estate Law prepares each participant's legal documents and returns for a follow-up session to complete the signing. By working together, groups gain access to professional legal advice at a significantly reduced cost.

These two-hour workshops, led by an experienced lawyer, give groups across Nova Scotia an affordable way to access professional estate planning services. Designed for unions, workplaces, churches, and nonprofits, the workshops guide participants through a straightforward process to have their Last Will and Testament, Power of Attorney, and Personal Directive professionally drafted.

Participants complete a questionnaire during the session, and Ross Estate Law prepares their legal documents afterward. The lawyer then returns for a follow-up visit to review and finalize the documents. By working as a group, participants receive personalized legal services at a fraction of the typical cost of hiring a lawyer individually.
