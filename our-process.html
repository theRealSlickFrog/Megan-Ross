Our Estate Planning Process

We've designed a clear, step-by-step process that takes the uncertainty out of estate planning. From your first appointment to ongoing support, we guide you through each stage with transparency and care.

1

BOOK APPOINTMENT ONLINE

We will send you an email with a list of matters to consider before our first meeting.

2

PLANNING MEETING

At our planning meeting we will make a plan for your estate, discuss next steps and book a signing.

3

DRAFT REVIEW

Ross Estate Law will send a draft of your documents one week prior to the signing appointment. If you want to discuss them, you can book a call the week before signing.

4

SIGNING

At our second meeting all documents will be finalized and signed. Payment is due at this meeting.

SECURE STORAGE

Ross Estate Law can store your will in a secure underground vault, in a guarded off-site storage unit. There is no additional fee for this service.

6

ONGOING REVISIONS

Every five years Ross Estate Law will contact you to see if you would like to update any of your documents. Clients with Ross Estate Law have a 50% discount on revisions to estate documents drafted by our offices.
